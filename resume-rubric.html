Content & Descriptions
Descriptions are specific, professional, and paint a full picture of abilities.

Consistency & Conventions
Follows professional conventions precisely and consistently throughout.
Mostly follows conventions but has noticeable inconsistencies in one or more areas.
Does not follow professional conventions, making the resume harder to read/credible.
