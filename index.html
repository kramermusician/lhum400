Real-World Readiness Audit

LHUM400  ·  Week 1  ·  Summer 2026

This is a 20-question self-audit covering the practical skills, systems, and decisions you will need within twelve months of leaving Berklee. There are no right answers here, only honest ones.

Rate yourself 1 to 5 on each question. At the end you will get a Readiness Score, a per-domain breakdown, a personalized action plan, and a resource toolkit you can download and keep.

This is not a graded assignment. It is a private map of your starting point. We will re-take this survey in Week 12 and compare.
