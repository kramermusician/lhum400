11:00 to 11:12  ·  Play

The 5-second test.

I put a few real musician sites on screen, five seconds each. That is how long a booker actually looks.

  • 1 Snap judgment. After five seconds: do you know who they are and what they do? Hire or skip?
  • 2 Name it. What made the good ones work? What made the bad ones lose you?
Nobody reads your site. They scan it. Keep that in mind for your hire-me page later today.

05
11:22 to 12:07  ·  Group Task

Career Intel Board. Pick your track.

Track A

Industry Research Matrix.

  • Research roles, companies, or gig paths in your direction
  • Log pay range, entry barrier, network need, remote-friendly
  • Color-scale format to see your best-fit options at a glance
  • Good fit if you're still mapping the landscape
Track B

Outreach Tracker.

  • Log real contacts: name, how you know them, next step
  • Track date of last contact and status (cold, warm, active)
  • Rule-based format: overdue turns red, warm turns green
  • Good fit if you already have people you should be reaching

Same spreadsheet skills either way. Pick whichever is actually useful to you right now.

06
Group task, how it runs

Groups of 3 to 4, by interest.

  • 1 Find your group: cluster by the career direction or track you're most curious about.
  • 2 Pick Track A or B and open a new Google Sheet, shared with everyone in the group.
  • 3 Research and fill in real rows, at least one entry per person.
  • 4 Apply conditional formatting: Format > Conditional formatting. Use the cheat card at your table.
  • 5 If your group has someone who missed last week, they drive the keyboard for the formatting step.
This isn't a worksheet you throw away. Keep the link. You can reopen this in August and it still works for you.

12:15 to 12:21  ·  Learn

The only five sections you need.

You already wrote most of this

The core four.

  • Home and about: who you are in one breath
  • Work: a few strong samples, not everything
  • Hire me: your services and rates
  • Contact: one clear way to reach you
Nice to have

The fifth.

  • Resume, as a download
  • Pull your about from your artist statement
  • Pull hire-me from your rates doc
  • Skip anything you cannot fill well yet

A clean one-page site beats an empty five-page one. Quality over quantity.

08
12:21 to 12:27  ·  Choose

Pick your tool, then stop shopping.

  • 1 Fastest to live: Carrd or Google Sites. A real one-pager in one class. Free or nearly free.
  • 2 More room to grow: Wix or Squarespace. More power, more polish, a little slower to launch.
  • 3 You code: plain HTML and GitHub Pages. Total control, only if you already know how.
There is no wrong choice. The wrong move is spending the whole class deciding. Pick in two minutes and start building.
