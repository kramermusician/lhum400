Stuff to Track

A workshop cabinet for the things you can't drop. Hover a folder to open it. Hover a drawer and press X to delete it. Keep separate cabinets as workbenches and switch between them up top. Your changes save to this browser.

How to use
  • Hover a folder to peek at it · click the folder label to expand it full-size
  • Click + Add item at the bottom of an open folder to add a new one
  • Double-click an item to rename it · click the × next to it to delete
  • Click the ✎ on an item to open a note for it. Write as much as you want; it saves automatically. A filled ✎ means that item has a note.
  • Click Edit on a folder to change its label, items, width, or color
  • Grab the Move handle (top-left) and drag a drawer to reorder it. An orange bar shows where it will snap in.
  • Hover a folder and press X to delete the whole drawer
  • Workbenches are saved sets of drawers. Use the tabs at the top to switch between them, click + New workbench to start a fresh set, click the active tab to rename it, or the × on a tab to delete it
  • All changes save automatically to your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
